Our Travel History

On a hot sunny day in Malaysia, Timothy Ng was feeling overwhelmed coming up with a travel itinerary for a trip to Tokyo, Japan with his girlfriend. Hence, he came up with a simple idea - one click, and get a travel plan set up instantly.

What started out as a simple idea evolved into a business plan with his co-founders to help travellers around the world save time and effort planning their trip. Whether it is a major tourist attraction, a famous restaurant or a hidden local gem, Travora is your one-stop solution in giving people what they want, whenever they want it.

Travora creates a starter travel itinerary carefully curated based on your travel preference from thousands of tourist attractions around the world. It rummages through large databases using an advanced algorithm to find the perfect itinerary for your travel destination.

The founders of the company are connected through a shared passion of travelling. Travora is built for the sole purpose of making the planning process easier for everyone and offers a complete travelling experience. So pack your bags, get ready, and set out on your own adventure!
